4. User Interaction and Workflows


The MotorPH Payroll System is a crucial component of the organization's administrative infrastructure, designed to manage and process employee compensation efficiently. Its primary objective is to accurately calculate employee salaries, deductions, and benefits in accordance with company policies and regulatory requirements. By automating payroll processes, the system aims to streamline payroll administration, reduce errors, and ensure timely and compliant disbursement of employee wages. Additionally, the Payroll System serves as a centralized repository for employee payroll data, facilitating reporting, analysis, and decision-making by stakeholders within the organization.


4.1 User Log in


Employeesâ€™ daily time registration will be done within the system after successfully completing login. A toggle button for time-in and time-out is displayed on the employee dashboard.


4.1.1 To log into the payroll system:


  1. Open the MotorPH Payroll System desktop application.
  2. Enter Employee ID and Password.
  3. Click Login.


4.2 Time Keeping


4.2.1 To record time-in after login:


  1. Click the Time in button on the dashboard.
  2. Click OK on the success alert.
  3. The timestamp of the time-in will be displayed on the timekeeping panel, and the button will change to Time out.


4.2.2 To record time-out:


  1. Click the Time out button on the dashboard.
  2. Click Yes to confirm or No to cancel.
  3. Click OK on the success alert.
  4. The timestamp of the time-in will be displayed on the timekeeping panel, and the button will change to Time in.



4.3 Viewing Profile Information


Employees utilizing the payroll system has the ability to view their information through the profile page after successfully logging in.


4.3.1 To view the profile information:


  1. Click Profile on the left sidebar menu.
  2. The profile panel will display employee information and provide an option for the user to change their password.


4.4 Changing the Password


Employees are encouraged to immediately updated their password on the first login and to regularly make changes to ensure account security. The ability to change the password is available on the profile page.


4.4.1 To change the user password after login:


  1. Click Profile on the left sidebar menu.
  2. Click the Change Password button.
  3. Enter the new password and enter it again to confirm in the Change Password dialog box.
  4. Click the Update Password button to save the changes.



4.5 Viewing Attendance Records


The payroll system provides a platform for the employees to view their attendance records, including overtime hours incurred. The attendance records can be viewed in the Attendance page


4.5.1 To view the attendance records after login:


  1. Click Attendance on the left sidebar menu.
  2. The attendance panel will display employee attendance records showing dates, timestamps of time in and time out, and overtime hours.



4.6 Overtime Application


Within the payroll system, all employees have the ability to request approval for overtime work. This feature can be accessed in the Attendance page, in the Overtime Request panel. Overtime requests are recorded by the system and displayed in a dedicated panel under the Attendance page.


4.6.1 Requesting Overtime Work:


  1. Click Attendance on the left sidebar.
  2. Click the Overtime Request tab.
  3. Click the Request Overtime button.
  4. Enter the Date when overtime work happens.
  5. Enter the Start Time and End Time.
  6. Enter the Reason for the overtime work.
  7. Click Submit Request.
  8. Click Yes to confirm or No to cancel.
  9. The overtime request will be displayed on the overtime request table with a Pending status as the employee awaits approval.



4.6.2 Viewing Overtime Request Records:


  1. Click Attendance on the left sidebar menu.
  2. Click the Overtime Request List button to view the list of the overtime requests initiated by the employee.
  3. The overtime requests table includes the date, start time, end time, and the status of the overtime request.


4.7 Leave Application


The payroll system provides a channel where the employees can submit their leave requests conveniently. The leave application can be accessed in the Leave page. The type of leave of absences in this application are: Vacation Leave, Sick Leave, and Emergency Leave.


4.7.1 Requesting Leave:


  1. Click Leave on the left sidebar.
  2. Click the Request Leave button.
  3. Select the type of leave from the Leave Type dropdown.
  4. Enter the Start Date and the End Date of the leave.
  5. Enter the Reason for the leave.
  6. Click Submit.
  7. Click OK on the success alert.
  8. The leave request will be displayed on the leave request table with a Pending status as the employee awaits approval.



4.7.2 Viewing Leave Request Records:


  1. Click Leave on the left sidebar.
  2. Click the Leave List button.
  3. The Leave Request History table is automatically populated with the leave request information including the date the request was filed, type of leave, start date, end date, reason, and status.



4.8 Viewing the Payslip


The payroll system offers a platform for the employees to view the details of their income, along with the breakdown of their earnings, benefits, deductions, and tax amount.


4.8.1 To view the payslip:


  1. Click Payslip on the left sidebar.
  2. Choose a cut-off from the available data.
  3. Click the View button to view the payslip generated for the chosen cut-off dates.
  4. To view payslips from other cutoff dates, repeat Step 2 and 3.



4.8.2 To download the payslip as PDF:


  1. In the payslip viewer, click the Print button in the top-left part of the window.
  2. Ensure that Microsoft Print to PDF is set as the Printer.
  3. Click the OK button.
  4. Select an appropriate location to save the PDF.
  5. Input an appropriate file name and confirm to save.



4.9 Approving/Denying Overtime Requests


Only authorized users, such as the supervisor, are allowed to approve or deny overtime requests. Changes to the status of the request are updated real-time.


4.9.1 To approve an overtime request after successful login as a supervisor:


  1. Click the Manage Requests button on the dashboard.
  2. Choose a cut-off from the available data.
  3. Overtime Requests are displayed first by default on the Requests screen.
  4. Locate the Pending request.
  5. Click the status of the request.
  6. Select Approved from the dropdown.
  7. Click the Save button at the bottom of the screen.
  8. Select Yes to confirm the approval.



4.9.2 To deny an overtime request after successful login as a supervisor:


  1. Click the Manage Requests button on the dashboard.
  2. Choose a cut-off from the available data.
  3. Overtime Requests are displayed first by default on the Requests screen.
  4. Locate the Pending request..
  5. LClick the status of the request.
  6. Select Denied from the dropdown.
  7. Click the Save button at the bottom of the screen.
  8. Select Yes to confirm the denial.
  9. Select Yes to confirm the approval.



4.10 Approving/Denying Leave Requests


The ability to approve or deny a leave request is accessible only to authorized users. Supervisors are authorized to approve leave requests. Changes to the status of the request are updated real-time.


4.10.1 To approve a leave request after successful login as a supervisor:

4.10.2 To deny a leave request after successful login as a supervisor:

  3. Locate the Pending request.
  4. Click the status of the request.
  5. Select Denied from the dropdown.

4.11 Managing Employee Information


Authorized users have the ability to manage employee information through the payroll system. HR Administrators can access the Manage Employee Information feature through the dashboard. Managing employee information includes adding, updating, and deleting individual employee information, and also importing new employee information in bulk.


4.11.1 Accessing the Manage Employee Information Screen:


  1. Click the Employee Records button on the dashboard.
  2. The Employee Records screen is composed of an employee search bar, employee table, and action buttons at the bottom.



4.11.2 Employee Search:


  1. Click the search bar at the top of the screen.
  2. Type the employee ID to search.
  3. Click the Search button to initiate search.
  4. The employee table will be updated with the information of the employee searched by ID.



4.11.3 Adding New Employee Information:


  1. Click the Add button on the menu.
  2. Locate the CSV file that contains employee data. It can include one record or more, as long as it follows the order of data on the table.
  3. Click the Open button on the file explorer.
  4. The employee table will be updated with the newly added employee information.



4.11.4 Updating Employee Information:


  1. Select an employee from the employee table.
  2. Click the Update Employee button.
  3. Modify the fields that need to be updated.
  4. Click the Save button to save the changes.
  5. The employee table will be updated with the newly updated information.



4.11.5 Deleting Employee Information:


  1. Select an employee from the employee table.
  2. Click the Delete button at the bottom of the window
  3. Confirm to delete the employee information.
  4. Click OK on the success alert.
  5. The employee information is removed from the table.
